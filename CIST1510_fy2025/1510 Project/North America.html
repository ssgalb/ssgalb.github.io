Would Americans make concessions? The end result of a merger would require Congress to possibly repeal or amend The Permanent Appropriations Act of 1929. Repealing the The-Permanent-Apportionment-Act-of-1929 which would increase the number of members in the House of Representatives or come up with a new way of allocating Congressional Members.

Congress may also have to revisit the structure of American fiscal federalism to realign U.S. fiscal policy for the addition of Canada.
