Private Lessons

We make learning music fun! We offer private lessons for piano, guitar, and violin. Contact us today to schedule your first lesson for free!

  • No Long-Term Contracts
  • Flexible Schedule

Piano Lessons

We offer piano lessons for all ages, beginning at age three. Whether you are a beginner or have been playing the piano for a while, we have a program to suit your needs. Learn new techniques, theory, harmony, and tone. Learning how to play the piano helps develop hand- eye coordination adn self-confidence.

Guitar Lessons

We teach acoustic guitar, electric guitar, bass guitar, and ukulele. We have guitar lessons for beginners, intermediate players, and advanced players. Improve your skills with weekly lessons. Learn new tips and techniques from experienced instructors. We also have a rock bank progam that you can join!

Violin Lessons

We offer violin lessons for all ages, beginning at age six. Our violin lessons include a variety of music styles, from fiddle to classical. Our instructors will help you fine-tune techniques and accomplish you musical goals. We work with you to create a customized program, tailored to your needs.
