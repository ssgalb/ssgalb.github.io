About Me

I selected customs union as the topic for my final project because I have a strong interest in international trade and economic integration. Customs unions are a crucial aspect of global trade, as they facilitate the movement of goods and services between member countries by eliminating tariffs and other trade barriers. This promotes economic growth, enhances competitiveness, and fosters cooperation among nations. The added cost of currency conversion and customs check point delays are eliminated in a customs union, making trade more efficient and cost-effective. Creating a customs union can lead to increased trade flows, investment opportunities, and job creation within member countries.

For a customs union to function effectively, member countries must agree on a common external tariff and coordinate their trade policies. This requires a high level of political and economic cooperation among the members. Overall, I believe that customs unions play a vital role in promoting economic development and regional integration, and I am excited to explore this topic further in my final project.
